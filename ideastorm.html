Ideastorm Hackathon

Embark on an innovation odyssey at IdeaStorm, where ideas take flight and innovation knows no bounds. This event is your canvas to craft solutions, pushing the boundaries of technology while embracing the pillars of entrepreneurship, leadership, and innovation. Join forces with fellow visionaries, immerse yourself in the creative process, and compete for recognition and exciting prizes. IdeaStorm is not just a competition; it's an invitation to redefine what's possible and shape the future of tech innovations. Join us and turn your ideas into a revolution!

Guidelines

1. Theme & Scope: Participants are encouraged to align their projects with the overarching theme of the hackathon, promoting innovation and addressing real-world challenges. Project Idea can span any domains.
2. Participant can compete individually or as a team of max 4 members.
3. Project Idea: Participants are encouraged to utilise emerging technologies to enhance the impact and feasibility of your solution.
4. Presentation: Prepare a compelling presentation highlighting your project's objectives, methodology, and potential impact.
5. Judging: Projects will be evaluated based on creativity, technical complexity, feasibility, and potential impact on society or a specific domain.
6. Submission: Submission guidelines will be provided closer to the end of the hackathon.
7. Enjoy the Experience: Most importantly, have fun, learn, and enjoy the exhilarating experience of transforming your ideas into impactful projects.
