Capture The Flag

Whether your cyber security enthusiast, ethical hacker or coding wizard the CTF welcomes all! It's a cyber odyssey where sharp minds navigate through complex puzzles, exploit vulnerabilities, and race against the clock to claim the desired Flag!!

Guidelines

1. Eligibility: Open to all participant, regardless of their level of background.
2. Participant can compete individually or as a team of 2.
3. Challenge Categories (areas): Cryptography; Web Exploitation; Forensics; Steganography; Reverse Engineering.
4. Scoring: Specific point value based on its difficulty level.
5. Flag Submission: Participant must submit the flags via designated submission platform and in the correct format specified.
6. Fair Play: Any form of cheating, including sharing flags or solutions, will result in disqualification.
7. Respectful Behaviour: Participant must treat each other and the coordinators with respect.
